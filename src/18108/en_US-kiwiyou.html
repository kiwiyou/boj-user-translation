C.E. 1998? Nah, Nirvana 2541

Statement

Arriving at Suvarnabhumi Airport to participate in ICPC Bangkok Regional, Team Redshift was surprised to see the large screen indicating the year as 2562.

Thai uses Buddhist calendar, which is based on the year Buddha entered Nirvana. Given the Buddhist year, convert into the Common Era year.

Input

An integer y, denotes the Buddhist year, is given on the first line. (1000 ≤ y ≤ 3000)

Output

Print an integer indicating the year in the Common Era.
