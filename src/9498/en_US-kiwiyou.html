Grade

Statement

Given your point on the exam (integer, in percentage), print the grade you are in. A: 90% ~ 100%, B: 80% ~ 89%, C: 70% ~ 79%, D: 60% ~ 69%, F: otherwise.

Input

An integer p, denoting the point on the exam, is given on the first line. (0 ≤ p ≤ 100)

Output

Print the grade you are in.
